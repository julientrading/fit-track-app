Description

The bench press is a compound upper-body exercise that primarily targets the pectoralis major (chest), while also working the anterior deltoids (shoulders) and triceps brachii. It's one of the most effective exercises for building upper body strength and mass.

The movement involves lying on a flat bench and pressing a barbell or dumbbells away from your chest until your arms are fully extended, then lowering it back down in a controlled manner.
